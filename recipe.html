Liuwi's Daily Special Recipe

Beef N Beans Burritos

What you will need:
- 2 tablespoons Alfa One rice bran oil
- 1 brown onion, finely chopped
- 2 garlic cloves, crushed
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- 2 teaspoons ground cumin
- 500g lean beef mince
- 2 tablespoons tomato paste
- 410g can crushed tomatoes
- 125g can corn kernels, drained
- 400g can kidney beans, drained, rinsed
- 1 cup cooked long grain white rice
- 1 cup grated tasty cheese
- guacamole and sour cream, to serve

Method:

1. Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a saucepan over medium heat.
2. Add onion and cook for 6 minutes until softened. Add Garlic and capsicum
3. Cook for 4 minutes, Add oregano, coriander, cumin and chili powder, cook stirring for a minute.
4. Heat oil in pan in medium to high heat, add Mince cook and stir for 6-8 minutes.
5. Return onion mixture to pan, stir to combine, and add tomato paste, stir for 1 minute and add crushed tomatoes.
6. Simmer for 10 minutes and add corn and Kidney beans, simmer for 10 minutes until veggies are tender, and season with salt and pepper
7. Transfer onto cooling rack and leave to cool, do not remove until they are cold.

Plating up:

1. Place 1 tortilla on a surface, top with 2 tablespoons of rice add one-sixth mince mixture.

2. Sprinkkle with 2 tablespoons of cheese. Roll up to enclose filling.

3. Repeat with remaining tortillas, rice, mince mixture and cheese.

Serve with guacamole and sour cream
